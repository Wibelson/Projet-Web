Our hotel

The Cap Lamandou Hotel is the property of Groupe Simact Inc. Built in 2003, the hotel is located in a quiet area of Lamandou in the tourist town of Jacmel, a five-minute drive from the historic center and a seven-minute drive from the airport. It is famous in the region for its luxurious comfort, appealing features and exceptional views of the Caribbean Sea.

The hotel has 32 spacious rooms, including 4 suites, featuring a simple and elegant decor. The restaurant provides a diverse and delicious menu and can accommodate over 100 people. The hotel is certified as secure and did not undergo any damage during the January 2010 earthquake.

The Cap Lamandou hotel also provides:

  • Wi-Fi in every room and in common areas
  • A free private parking space
  • Round-the-clock reception service to guide you and answer all your questions
  • Baggage service
  • Room service
  • Dry-cleaning service
  • Medical contact service
  • Excursion booking service
  • A public beach at the foot of the hotel
  • A well-equipped gym for your training exercises
  • A spa and professional masseuses for your well-being
  • A multifunction room for your meetings
